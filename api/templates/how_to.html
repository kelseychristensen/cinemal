Cinemal

Play

Cinemal is a movie guessing game with a new answer, a popular movie, each day. Type your first guess into the search bar, and select your guess from the search results.

Cinemal will tell you if any actors, directors, writers, or studios from your guess appeared in or worked on the answer movie.

Cinemal will also tell you if the release year of your guess is before, or after the answer movie.

You have unlimited guesses, but the number of guesses it takes you to reach the answer will affect your overall player stats.
